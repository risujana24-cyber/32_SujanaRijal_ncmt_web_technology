"Planning is the most critical phase of web development.
Discussion
The statement is true as it sets a foundation for the entire project.
Information Gathering: This involves detailed discussions with the client to understand their vision, business goals, target audience demographics
and functional requirements.
Requirement Analysis: The gathered information is analyzed to define specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) project requirements.
Sitemap Creation: A sitemap is developed to define the website's structure, hierarchy, and navigation flow, ensuring a logical user experience
and comprehensive content coverage.
Wireframing and Prototyping: Basic visual layouts (wireframes) and interactive models (prototypes) are created to visualize the interface 
and user flow, allowing for early feedback and revisions.
Technology Stack Selection: The appropriate technologies, platforms, programming languages, and tools are chosen based on project
requirements and scalability needs.
Budgeting and Timeline: A detailed project schedule, milestones, and budget allocation are established to manage expectations and 
resources effectively.
Risk Management: Potential technical, operational, or security risks are identified, and mitigation strategies are planned 
to prevent project delays or failures.
